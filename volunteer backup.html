How it works ?

Step : 1

Form a Group or a Co-op for your Local Vendors

Local Vendor Co-ops or Associations of local vendors, Muncipal Corporations, Government Agencies, Institues and Office Campuses are our preferred local parnters. If you do not belong to the above category please get in touch with us and talk to us !

Dont worry about Legal Requirments. You can Join our platform before finishing the legal registration process.

Step : 2

Install and Setup your local market

You can self-host your Instance or also choose from various managed hosting plans. Create Self-Hosted Explore Managed Hosting Plans

To self-host your Instance (Server) on Digital Ocean and AWS. Please follow our installation guide from our developer guide https://developer.nearbyshops.org

Step : 3

Receive Orders and Provide Home Delivery

You will receive orders from customers. Delivery of the orders can be provided by the vendor himself or by the delivery staff of the cooperative.

Step : 4

Ask Donations or Charge a Fee to fund your expenses

In order to fund expenses of hosting of your local market and pay the salary for your staff you can ask for donations or Charge a fixed Fee per order to your local vendors.
