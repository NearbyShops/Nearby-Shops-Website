How it works?

Step 1 : Create your Local Market

Anyone can create a Local Market. Your Local Market is now accessible from Nearby Shops app.

Step 2 : Gain Free access to our Customers

Save your marketing expenses by getting free access to existing Customers of Nearby Shops app. Customers can send orders directly from our app.

Step 3 : Invite Vendors to Join your Market

Vendors will receive orders from Existing customers of our app. Delivery is provided by the vendors therefore you dont need to provide delivery.

Step 4 : Earn Income from your Market (Optional)

As a market admin you can add multiple Vendors. Charge your vendors a fixed monthly fee to earn income from your market.
