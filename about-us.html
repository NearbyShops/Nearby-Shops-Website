About us

Nearby Shops is widely acclaimed open-source project. We aim to provide a viable non-commercial alternative to food delivery and grocery business. Nearby Shops is developed with a goal of helping the local vendors and local economy.

Our goal is to improve the lives of our Vendors and Provide them better income. You can also call Nearby Shops a Nonprofit Alternative to Grubhub, Instacart and Zomato.

We partner with vendors and cooperatives across the world and work with them to provide food delivery and local shopping services.

Our Story and Purpose

Nearby Shops started out as an open-source project. Featured on front page of Hacker News two times. Nearby Shops is widely appreciated in the developer community.

Local economy and small businesses are the backbone of our economy. We see that most of the big tech organizations are not doing enough to help them.

This project aims to help the local vendors and local economy by being a viable non-commercial alternative to the Commercial E-commerce platforms.

Our Values and Our Culture

We believe the best innovations happen when the community is allowed to grow organically in a zero fear environment. We want to help create a culture that nurtures human values and cares about people and relationships.

Therefore we belive in Open Source and we want to keep our platform open for all. We try to build relationships based on trust and faith. Which enables us to allow our employees to work from home and have flexible working hours. And benefit from a perfect work life balance.

We want people to be driven by their interests not by fear. And we want to create a community where people feel loved and cared instead of being manipulated and controlled.

As volunteers we are just there to help and provide the technology and we believe in the cooperation and community participation to decide whats best for people and our community.
