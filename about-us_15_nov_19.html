About us

Nearby Shops is developed with a goal of helping the local vendors and local economy.

It runs on open-source technology.

The end-users can access multiple local markets from a single mobile app and login account. This means that there is one single mobile app for all the local markets. It helps in saving the marketing efforts and costs.

These local markets are self-hosted and managed by independent local vendor cooperatives who seek the welfare of their local vendors.

Our Story and Purpose

Local economy and small business is the backbone of our economy. We see that most of the big tech organizations are not doing enough to help them.

This project aims to help the local vendors and local economy with the help of technology.

Our Values

This project simply aims to provide the technology and let the community use it the way they want.

We believe the best innovations happen when the community is allowed to grow organically so we are against putting any restrictions on our community.

As volunteers we are just there to help and provide the technology and we believe it is not our responsibility to decide whats best for people and our community.

We decided its best to fund this project using donations because it helps us keep it ethical and free.
